Release notes

 

jGallery1.2 - PHP based photo album viewer V1

 

You can view a live demo of jGallery at; http://portal.kooijman-design.nl/jGallery/

 

 

 

Download, installation & support forums:

You can visit the forums at; http://portal.kooijman-design.nl/viewforum.php?f=1

 

 

Installation:

jGallery requires PHP5 and GD-library to be installed on your server.

 

Upload all files in the *.zip-package to your server, after this you can simply upload images and folders to the ‘albums/’-directory.

 

In the config.inc.php file you can configure al lot of things which change the looks of your album.

 

 

Features:

jGallery provides you with a menu with which you can brows your photo’s online. The menu shows you your current position and you can always switch back home.

 

You can change folderimages, cols and rows in your gallery and thumbnail sizes.

 

jGalery can be included from a higher directory and will function the same. Links will appear the same as the rest of your pages style. When not included, the stulesheet in the package is used. Some style-options are to be set in config.inc.php.

 

 

Usage:

To include jGallery in another page simply use this line:

<?php include('jGallery/index.php'); ?>

 

By editing config.inc.php you can tune jGallery up to fit into your website’s template exactly.

 

 

Enjoy!
